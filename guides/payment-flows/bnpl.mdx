## Overview

Buy Now Pay Later (BNPL) allows customers to split their purchase into installments with a third-party provider. The customer is redirected to the BNPL provider's page to approve the financing.

## Payment Flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create payment">
    ```json
    {
      "checkout_session": "session-id",
      "payment_method": {"type": "BNPL"},
      "amount": {"currency": "BRL", "value": 500.00},
      "country": "BR",
      "customer": {
        "document": {"document_type": "CPF", "document_number": "12345678901"},
        "email": "customer@example.com"
      }
    }
    ```
  </Step>

  <Step title="Redirect customer">
    The response includes a redirect URL. The customer completes the BNPL application on the provider's page.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Handle callback">
    After the customer completes or cancels, they are redirected back to your site. Use [webhooks](/guides/webhooks/overview) for the definitive payment status.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
BNPL availability depends on provider configuration and may vary by country. Check your Dashboard for enabled BNPL providers.
</Note>
