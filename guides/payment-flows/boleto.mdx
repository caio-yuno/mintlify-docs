## Overview

Boleto bancario is a popular payment method in Brazil. A payment voucher (boleto) is generated for the customer to pay at banks, ATMs, or online banking.

## Prerequisites

- Country: `BR`, Currency: `BRL`
- Customer document: CPF (11 digits) or CNPJ (14 digits) required
- Boleto enabled in Dashboard

## Payment Flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create payment">
    ```json
    {
      "checkout_session": "session-id",
      "payment_method": {"type": "BOLETO"},
      "amount": {"currency": "BRL", "value": 200.00},
      "country": "BR",
      "customer": {
        "document": {"document_type": "CPF", "document_number": "12345678901"},
        "email": "customer@example.com",
        "first_name": "John",
        "last_name": "Doe"
      }
    }
    ```
  </Step>

  <Step title="Display boleto to customer">
    The response includes:
    - Barcode image
    - Digitable line (numeric code for online banking)
    - Expiration date
    - PDF URL for printing
  </Step>

  <Step title="Await payment">
    Boletos typically have a 1-3 day expiration window. Set up [webhooks](/guides/webhooks/overview) to receive payment confirmation when the customer pays.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Boleto payments can take 1-3 business days to be confirmed after the customer pays. Factor this into your order fulfillment process.
</Note>
