## Overview

OXXO is Mexico's largest convenience store chain. Customers receive a payment voucher with a barcode and pay with cash at any OXXO location.

## Prerequisites

- Country: `MX`, Currency: `MXN`
- Customer email required
- OXXO enabled in Dashboard

## Payment Flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create payment">
    ```json
    {
      "checkout_session": "session-id",
      "payment_method": {"type": "OXXO"},
      "amount": {"currency": "MXN", "value": 500.00},
      "country": "MX",
      "customer": {
        "email": "customer@example.com"
      }
    }
    ```
  </Step>

  <Step title="Display voucher">
    The response includes a barcode and reference number. Display or email these to the customer.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Await payment">
    The customer pays at an OXXO store. Set up [webhooks](/guides/webhooks/overview) for payment confirmation. OXXO payments can take up to 24 hours to be confirmed.
  </Step>
</Steps>
