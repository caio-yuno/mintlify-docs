## Merchant Category Codes (MCC)

MCC codes classify merchants by the type of goods or services they provide. These codes may be required for specific provider configurations.

| MCC | Category | Description |
|-----|----------|-------------|
| `5411` | Grocery Stores | Supermarkets and grocery stores |
| `5541` | Service Stations | Gas stations and fuel |
| `5812` | Restaurants | Eating places and restaurants |
| `5814` | Fast Food | Quick service restaurants |
| `5912` | Drug Stores | Pharmacies |
| `5942` | Book Stores | Book retailers |
| `5944` | Jewelry Stores | Jewelry and watch shops |
| `5999` | Miscellaneous Retail | General retail |
| `7011` | Hotels | Lodging and hotels |
| `7372` | Software | Computer programming and software |
| `7399` | Business Services | Miscellaneous business services |
| `7832` | Motion Pictures | Movie theaters |
| `7922` | Entertainment | Theatrical producers and events |
| `7995` | Gambling | Betting and casino |
| `8011` | Doctors | Physicians and medical |
| `8021` | Dentists | Dental services |
| `8062` | Hospitals | Healthcare facilities |
| `8111` | Legal Services | Attorneys and legal |
| `8211` | Schools | Elementary and secondary schools |
| `8299` | Educational Services | Other educational services |

<Note>
This is a subset of commonly used MCC codes. Contact Yuno support if you need a category not listed here.
</Note>
