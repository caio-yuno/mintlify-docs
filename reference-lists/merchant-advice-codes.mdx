## Merchant Advice Codes

Merchant Advice Codes (MAC) are returned by card networks when a transaction is declined. These codes provide guidance on whether and how to retry the transaction.

## Visa MAC Codes

| Code | Description | Action |
|------|-------------|--------|
| `01` | New account information available | Update card details and retry |
| `02` | Cannot approve at this time, try again later | Retry after delay |
| `03` | Do not retry | Do not retry — permanent decline |
| `21` | Payment cancellation — customer requested | Cancel recurring billing |
| `24` | Retry after 72 hours | Wait at least 72 hours before retry |
| `25` | Retry after 30 days | Wait at least 30 days before retry |
| `26` | Retry after 60 days | Wait at least 60 days before retry |
| `27` | Retry after 90 days | Wait at least 90 days before retry |

## Mastercard MAC Codes

| Code | Description | Action |
|------|-------------|--------|
| `01` | New account information available | Request updated card data |
| `02` | Cannot approve at this time | Retry with exponential backoff |
| `03` | Do not retry | Permanent decline — do not retry |
| `04` | Token requirements not fulfilled | Check token validity |
| `21` | Payment cancellation | Cancel recurring billing |
| `24` | Retry after minimum 1 hour | Wait before retry |
| `25` | Retry after minimum 1 day | Wait before retry |
| `26` | Retry after minimum 2 days | Wait before retry |
| `27` | Retry after minimum 4 days | Wait before retry |
| `28` | Retry after minimum 8 days | Wait before retry |

## Decline Categories

| Category | Typical Response Codes | Description |
|----------|----------------------|-------------|
| **Soft Decline** | `02`, `24`-`28` | Temporary issue — retry is appropriate |
| **Hard Decline** | `03`, `21` | Permanent issue — do not retry |
| **Data Quality** | `01`, `04` | Card data needs updating |

<Warning>
Card networks enforce retry limits. Exceeding retry limits for hard declines can result in fines from the card network. Always respect MAC codes.
</Warning>
