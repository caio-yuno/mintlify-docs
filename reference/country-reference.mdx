## Overview

This reference provides a unified matrix of every country supported by Yuno, covering payment method availability, required customer fields, provider coverage, regulatory obligations, card brand support, and settlement calendars. Use this document to plan integrations, validate payloads, and understand compliance requirements per market.

<Note>
Actual payment method and provider availability depends on your merchant configuration and agreements. Enable providers in [Dashboard > Connections](/platform/dashboard/connections). Data in this document reflects general platform capabilities and may vary by account.
</Note>

#### Regulatory Requirements

- **3DS:** Recommended but not mandated by regulation. Networks (Visa, MC) may mandate on high-risk transactions.
- **KYC/AML:** BACEN (Central Bank) requires CPF/CNPJ for all electronic payments. PIX transactions require tax ID for sender and receiver.
- **Transaction Limits:** PIX: configurable per institution (typical default R$1,000 nocturnal limit for individuals). Boleto: no regulatory cap but issuer limits apply.
- **Data Residency:** LGPD (Lei Geral de Protecao de Dados) applies. Personal data of Brazilian residents must follow LGPD consent and processing rules.
- **Installments:** Credit cards support 2-12 installments (issuer-dependent). Interest-free installments require merchant agreement.

- **Data Residency:** LFPDPPP (Federal Law on Protection of Personal Data) applies.

| Country | Data Privacy Law | 3DS Requirement | KYC Document | Reporting Threshold | Currency Controls |
|---------|-----------------|-----------------|--------------|--------------------|--------------------|
| BR | LGPD | Recommended (not mandated) | CPF / CNPJ | COAF reporting; thresholds set by BACEN | Limited; BACEN oversight on FX |
| MX | LFPDPPP | Not mandated | RFC / CURP | LFPIORPI: ~MXN $50,000 | Limited; Banxico oversight |
| CO | Law 1581 (Habeas Data) | Not mandated | CC / CE / NIT | UIAF: ~COP $10,000,000 | Moderate; Banco de la Republica controls |
| AR | Law 25,326 | Not mandated | DNI / CUIT / CUIL | UIF: adjusted periodically (inflation) | Strict; BCRA "cepo cambiario" on USD |
| CL | Law 19,628 | Not mandated | RUT | UAF: ~UF 450 | Limited; BCCh oversight |
| PE | Law 29,733 | Not mandated | DNI / RUC / CE | UIF-Peru: PEN 10,000+ | Limited; BCRP oversight |
| UY | Law 18,331 | Not mandated | CI / RUT | UIAF: thresholds set by BCU | Limited; BCU oversight |
| US | State laws (CCPA, etc.) | Network-dependent (Visa/MC rules) | Not typically required | BSA/FinCEN: $10,000+ | None |
| CA | PIPEDA | Network-dependent | Not typically required | FINTRAC: CAD $10,000+ | None |

<Note>
3DS is not mandated by any LatAm regulator as of 2026. However, card networks (Visa, Mastercard) may enforce 3DS on specific transaction types, particularly cross-border. Enabling 3DS shifts fraud liability to the issuer and typically improves approval rates for international cards.
</Note>

## Document Type Reference

Tax identification documents required for Latin American payment methods.

| Document Type | Code | Country | Description | Format |
|---------------|------|---------|-------------|--------|
| CPF | `CPF` | BR | Cadastro de Pessoas Fisicas (Individual tax ID) | 11 digits (e.g., `12345678901`) |
| CNPJ | `CNPJ` | BR | Cadastro Nacional da Pessoa Juridica (Business tax ID) | 14 digits (e.g., `12345678000199`) |
| CC | `CC` | CO | Cedula de Ciudadania (Citizen ID) | 6-10 digits |
| CE | `CE` | CO, PE | Cedula de Extranjeria (Foreign resident ID) | 6-7 digits (CO), 9 digits (PE) |
| NIT | `NIT` | CO, SV, GT | Numero de Identificacion Tributaria (Tax ID) | 9-10 digits |
| TI | `TI` | CO | Tarjeta de Identidad (Minor ID) | 10-11 digits |
| PP | `PP` | CO, PE, EC | Passport | Alphanumeric, varies by issuing country |
| DNI | `DNI` | AR, PE, HN | Documento Nacional de Identidad (National ID) | AR: 7-8 digits, PE: 8 digits |
| CUIT | `CUIT` | AR | Clave Unica de Identificacion Tributaria (Tax ID) | 11 digits (e.g., `20-12345678-9`) |
| CUIL | `CUIL` | AR | Clave Unica de Identificacion Laboral (Labor ID) | 11 digits |
| RUT | `RUT` | CL, UY | Rol Unico Tributario (Tax ID) | CL: 8-9 digits + check digit, UY: 12 digits |
| RUC | `RUC` | PE, EC, PY, PA | Registro Unico de Contribuyente (Tax registry) | PE: 11 digits, EC: 13 digits |
| RFC | `RFC` | MX | Registro Federal de Contribuyentes (Federal tax registry) | 12-13 alphanumeric characters |
| CURP | `CURP` | MX | Clave Unica de Registro de Poblacion (Population registry) | 18 alphanumeric characters |
| INE | `INE` | MX | Instituto Nacional Electoral (Voter ID) | 13 digits |
| CI | `CI` | BO, CR, DO, EC, NI, PA, PY, UY | Cedula de Identidad (Identity card) | Varies by country |
| DUI | `DUI` | SV | Documento Unico de Identidad | 9 digits |
| DPI | `DPI` | GT | Documento Personal de Identificacion | 13 digits |
| RTN | `RTN` | HN | Registro Tributario Nacional (Tax registry) | 14 digits |
| DIMEX | `DIMEX` | CR | Documento de Identidad Migratoria para Extranjeros | 11-12 digits |
| RNC | `RNC` | DO | Registro Nacional del Contribuyente (Tax registry) | 9 digits |

</Warning>
