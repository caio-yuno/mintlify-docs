## Overview

Yuno supports payment processing across Latin America and other regions through multiple providers. This matrix shows the available countries, currencies, and payment methods.

<Note>
Coverage depends on your configured providers. Enable providers in [Dashboard > Connections](/platform/dashboard/connections) to activate payment methods for specific countries.
</Note>

## Requesting New Coverage

To request a new country, payment method, or provider:

1. Contact your Yuno account manager or support
2. Specify the country, payment method, and expected volume
3. Yuno will assess provider availability and onboarding requirements
